About the 3D Art Gallery

Welcome to our virtual 3D Art Gallery. Here, you can navigate through a variety of wonderful artworks and learn about their history and significance.

The gallery is best experienced in full-screen mode. For optimal navigation, use the arrow keys or the W, A, S, and D keys. You can also use your mouse to change the viewing direction.

Controls

W/A/S/D: Move around

Mouse: Look around

Space: Toggle pointer lock

M: Show Menu

Enter: Start exploration

Esc: Stop exploration

G: Start audio guide

P: Stop audio guide
